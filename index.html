    About Me

    What have I done?

    I have worked with Emerson for over 2 years with their Hardware and Embedded Systems. I have worked (4 month internship) with R&D of Chamberlain Group (Liftmaster and Home Access Entry) as a Computer Engineer from planning to prototyping to building new consumer electronic products such as Modular Smart Home/Garage Entry System, Pet Portal Door, etc. I have also worked with R&D of Shure Incorporated as a Research Engineer for Embedded Software with their future audio devices such as Ceiling Array Microphones and Speakers. I have also worked with AMD Full Time as a Firmware ENgineer helping the Diagnostics team develop a Automated Testing Environment for testing CPUs and GPUs.
    During these experiences, I have worked with Mechanical Engineers since I'm familiar with Solidworks and AutoCAD and Software Engineers and Design Teams. I have worked with the testing of hardware devices and writing the code for communication protocols. I am also familiar with Altium and PCB layout for projects starting from scratch including digital and circuit design. I have also developed Android apps (using Java) for consumer electronic devices to communicate wirelessly using Bluetooth, Wifi etc. I have multiple Embedded Based projects with award winning professors. A few of them are Autonomous Vehicle/Robotic Smart Car, Smart Braille device for the visually impaired, etc.
